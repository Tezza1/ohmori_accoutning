Accounting Services Explained

YOU: Client collects their receipts and eneters them into a monthly Excel sheet.

US: We will show you how you best collect and record receipts.

All finished! Relax, and leave it to us!

US: We will create, complete and submit the required Annual Reports and Tax Returns to the Tax Office for you.

US: We will deal with any questions from the Tax Office regarding your financial documents.
